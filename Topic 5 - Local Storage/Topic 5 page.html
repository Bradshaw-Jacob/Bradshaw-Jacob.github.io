Local Storage Storing and Retrieving Arrays, Associative Arrays, and Objects

Topic 5 deals with local storage. Before HTML5 web pages had to use cookies to store information. With HTML 5 you can now use the local storage and session storage. These both allow you to store a lot more data then cookies ever could.

The local storage does not have an expiration date and will continue to hold the data until the storage is cleared.
