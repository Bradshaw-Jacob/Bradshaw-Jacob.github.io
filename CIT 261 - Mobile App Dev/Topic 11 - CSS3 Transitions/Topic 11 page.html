CSS3 Transitions

In this topic we will look at Transitions. Transitions are great and smooth changes between different element states. Transitions have similar properties as animations and transforms. The duration property is the only required property that you need to specify. With out a duration, the transition will not occur.

Here we have a div that will expand when you hover over it. This div does not have a transition so it will look very jumpy and does not look very good.
